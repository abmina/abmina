## ⚡ Beyond Copilot: Local-First AI
I’m the lead developer of **[Dark Matter IDE](https://github.com/abmina/dark-matter-ide)**, a VS Code fork built for privacy and performance. I believe your code and your AI should stay on your hardware.

* **Zero Subscriptions:** No "Token Tax" or monthly fees.
* **Airplane Mode:** Full agentic capabilities with zero internet connection.
* **Hardware Driven:** Optimized for local VRAM (Running on dual 2080 Tis).
